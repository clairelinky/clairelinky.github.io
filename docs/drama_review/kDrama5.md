## 5 stars

演員、劇情、攝影畫面，整體優秀，會想再看一次。

## Hospital Playlist 機智醫生生活 (2020)(2021)

Best korean drama ever.

## Lovestruck in the City 愛在大都會 (2020)

My favorite romance comedy.

## Because This Is My First Life 今生是第一次 (2017)

One of my favorite leading actress (Jung So-min) acts in this drama. Even other characters are not attractive, they didn't lower the score I gave to this drama.  

## Hometown Cha-Cha-Cha 海岸村恰恰恰 (2021)

All the characters have vivid personality. The story of everyone is present well. The plot goes smoothly and delightful.

## Extraordinary Attorney Woo 非常律師禹英禑 (2022)

It's cute and warm. I really enjoy it.

## Our Beloved Summer 那年，我們的夏天 (2021)

Every actor and actress performs so well. I also like the idea of the whole story.
